# PERSONAS — TaskFlow

## Persona 1: Sam the Solo Founder

### Demographics
- **Age range:** 28–40
- **Role:** Solo founder / freelancer
- **Tech comfort:** Intermediate
- **Device usage:** Desktop-primary, mobile for quick checks

### Goals
- Track what needs to be done today without opening a complex PM tool
- Feel in control of their workload

### Pain Points
- Jira/Asana feel like overkill for a 1-person team
- Apple Notes lacks structure — no due dates, no priorities
- Switching between too many tools wastes time

### Quote
> "I just want a clean list of what I need to do today, with deadlines I can trust."

---

## Persona 2: Alex the Small Team Lead

### Demographics
- **Age range:** 30–45
- **Role:** Team lead at a 3–5 person startup
- **Tech comfort:** High
- **Device usage:** Desktop

### Goals
- See what everyone is working on at a glance
- Assign tasks without a 20-minute setup process

### Pain Points
- Free tiers on existing tools are too limited for small teams
- Onboarding team members to complex tools takes days

### Quote
> "I need my team on the same page in 5 minutes, not 5 days."

---

## Anti-Persona
- Enterprise project managers with 50+ team members — they need Jira
- Developers who want GitHub Issues integration — not in v1
