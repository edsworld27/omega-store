## WHAT ARE THESE?

AI Assistants are pre-configured AI agents built for specific tasks. Unlike task files (which any AI can run), these are hosted AI tools with:

- Custom system prompts
- Specialized knowledge
- Specific workflows baked in
- Direct links to use them

---

## HOW TO USE

1. Click the link for the assistant you need
2. It opens in Claude / GPT / custom interface
3. Follow the assistant's instructions
4. Get your output
